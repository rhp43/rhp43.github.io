Player Safety in Youth Football

My masters thesis focusing on the grassroot efforts in youth football to keep athletes safe. Parents want coaches who are not just knowledgeable about the game, but able to teach fundamentals which focus on player safety. The president and coaches of the Xtreme Youth Football League in Miami, Fla. explain their efforts to keep their athletes safe.

Ricardo's Journey

"Ricardo's Journey" is the first sports documentary I produced and edited. Ricardo Salguero struggled with his weight throughout his life. I captured his efforts in changing his eating habits as well as his training regimen for obstacle races as he adopted a healthy lifestyle.
